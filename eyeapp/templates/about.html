About

Your Premier Destination for Comprehensive Eye Care

Founded in Loving Memory of Dr. Ramesh Kumar Kurup and His Daughter Sarada On October 27, 2006, Dr. Suhichithra, along with her sisters, Dr. Chithra Lekha and Anitha, established our eye care hospital. In honor of the legacy left by Dr. Ramesh Kumar Kurup, our hospital is dedicated to serving the underprivileged with unwavering commitment and providing high-quality eye care services to the community in and around Varkala. Our mission is rooted in compassion, excellence, and a vision for a healthier, brighter future for all.

Committed to exceptional eye care and cutting-edge treatments with a high level of expertise. We prioritize your vision and the well-being of every individual. With over 2,000 successful surgeries, our dedication to your eye health is unwavering.

At our eye care hospital, we excel in enhancing and preserving your vision, ensuring not only optimal eye health but also the radiance of your gaze. Recognizing the significance of vision treatments in your overall well-being, we understand the challenge of choosing the perfect eye care. Count on our outstanding team to provide unwavering support, assisting you comprehensively on your journey to clear and vibrant sight.
